Fire Prevention ways

  Keep a fire extinguisher at all times Having a fire extinghuisher in your house is very essential especially when your house has been unchecked. It is a good back up plan to have. But make sure to keep in a safe and accesible place. And change it when it is expired. A good alternative is a blanket.
  Maintain wires in your house In our houses we all have lots of devices and including their wires. We need to regularly check them and remove when not in use. An extra safety measure is to use surge protectors. And sometimes I know that we don't like wasting money on new wires but it is for our safety to replace and dispose our damaged wires as it poses a risk to our safety.
  Store any flammable products correctly We have lots of different flammable products and since they are flammabe of course we need to keep them in a safe enviroment to prevent fires.
  While cooking keep total focus When cooking it almost always involves fire. When we are cooking or someone in your house is cooking we need to have total focus or keep an eye on the pan with fire.
  Install smoke alarms in your house. If you can afford it you can install smoke alarms as a extra measure for prevention.
